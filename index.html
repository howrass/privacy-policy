LifeKit - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

LifeKit ("the App") is developed by howrass ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

LifeKit is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • No account registration is required.
  • No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted to any server.
  • All data (alarms, tasks, habits, diary entries, memos, settings, etc.) is stored locally on your device only.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are included in the App.

Internet Access

The App requires internet access solely for the following purpose:

  • Weather feature: The App connects to the Open-Meteo API (open-meteo.com) to retrieve weather forecast data for locations you manually add. Only latitude and longitude coordinates are sent to this service. No personal identifiers are transmitted.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device (except weather API requests as described above).
  • Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all stored data.
  • You can export a backup of your data as a ZIP file using the Backup feature, and restore it on another device.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Open-Meteo API: For weather data. Their privacy policy is available at open-meteo.com/en/terms.
  • Google Play In-App Review API: To optionally prompt users for app reviews. No personal data is collected by us through this API.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App does not require any personal information to function.

Advertising

LifeKit contains no advertisements of any kind. We do not use any advertising frameworks or SDKs.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
